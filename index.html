Welcome to AZ Winery.

Established in 1890 by Pedro Piazza and later acquired by the Lemos family, the AZ winery produces high quality fine wines. Located in the Agrelo region, in the department of Lujan de Cuyo, Mendoza. This winery is characterized by having a limited production of elegant wines that have the essence of the region. Over the years the winery has been modernized in order to meet the quality standards that today's world demands without losing the essence that made this winery so famous. Thanks to the efficiency in the use of water both in the agricultural stage and in the production process, it is possible to take care of water, which is a scarce resource in Mendoza. We create our wines to be enjoyed and shared with our loved ones

Enjoy our Wines.

The winery can be visited to appreciate its beautiful landscape. It is a unique experience to enjoy a good wine surrounded by the mountains. Through a guided tour you can learn from the inside how the production process is to end with a lunch inside the restaurant of the winery where typical dishes of the region will be tasted.
