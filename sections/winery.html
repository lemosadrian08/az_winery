Winery

Az Winery was built in 1890. It has a facade of the time which over the years has been restored to preserve the essence of the time, but adapted to today's standards. This is the result of years of investment to maintain this Mendoza heritage. the winery has the latest technology to produce high quality wine. It has both stainless steel equipment and French oak barrels that allow it to produce 2,000,000 liters of wine annually. Although this capacity is limited to maintain the exclusivity of our wines.
